How to subscribe (and send responses)

Two ways: Atom feed or Mastodon.

Atom

This blog delivers updates via Atom feeds, a sibling of RSS format. To use it, you need a reader – you can find some suggestions here.

I publish atom feeds for

  • All articles
  • Category: Blog
  • Category: Tech Talk

You can send responses via email or using the WebMentions-based interactions form at the end of the every article.

Mastodon

I often announce my blog posts on my Mastodon profile - where you could follow and even send responses.

For the uninitiated, Mastodon is a micro blog which talks to other servers like e-mail. More accurately, it is one among many software solutions which forms a network called Fediverse. There are several server instances you can join.

  • My Mastodon profile
  • Follow me with an account on Fediverse
  • Follow me without an account via RSS
